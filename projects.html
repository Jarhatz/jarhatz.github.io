Why do I code?

My motivation for coding comes from the inspiring works of other fellow engineers who continually push out new developments for the world to see. I am a strong believer in the notion that technology exists to make human life easier while also making the planet a better place for all inhabitants.

My thirst for deepening my understanding of Machine Learning and Computer Vision is fueled by my passion for the engulfing field of Artificial Intelligence. The potential applications from these areas of research and development feed the inner child in me who really wants to witness a world full of humans coexisting with AI. The projects I have completed so far (and the ones of the future) are all strong indicators of my interests as an engineer and a programmer.
